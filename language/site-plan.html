Content

Home page

My website will be attractive for people because of its topic. The languages learning is a fantastic world, and my website will provide information about languages and how to learn them. This website will be a space for those who love to learn different languages for different reasons (travel, culture, polyglotism, dating, etc.). One of the ideas I have in mind is to attract both beginners and advanced in the subject to help them providing some tips, tools and information to learn languages. For those who are beginners, this page will motivate them to start learning a new language giving them the benefits of doing it and helping to decide which language to learn and why according to their goals or/and dreams. It will also provide tips and useful information for advanced which can help them to improve their learning or methods. Languages are a system of communication that consist of grammar and vocabulary. This topic has many passionate people who like to learn as a hobby and my website brings together information on many languages (rankings, popularity, usefulness, complexity due to grammar or pronunciation, and more) that can make it easier for both learners and advanced people to learn more about certain languages and be able to decide which one attracts them the most and whether they choose to learn that one or not. Languages teach a lot about the culture and way of being of the countries and people who speak them. Therefore, you also learn a little about the cultures of many countries by learning their languages. There are many ways to learn in life, and in languages there are no exceptions. Not everyone learns in the same way or uses the same tools, although they do need to learn the same thing, the language. For this reason, on my website there will be a space dedicated to this point, which is how to learn, and will provide tips in many ways to learn languages. Some are easier to learn in a certain way and others in another. Learning what language and how to approach it will make learning it easier. In this space where information on how to learn will be provided, there will be tips depending on the language you want to learn and knowing how long it could take to acquire the necessary skills to speak that particular language. There is a term that perhaps not many know, polyglotism. There are communities of polyglots around the world whose purposes are always to share, motivate and inspire others to learn about the world of polyglotism. According to the Merriam-Webster Dictionary, polyglotism is: “the use of many languages” or “the ability to speak many languages”. Being a polyglot requires a lot of dedication and love for learning languages. When good fluency is achieved in a second language that has been learned, it can motivate us to continue seeking to learn another. And the best thing is that when you manage to master the first language, the others that you learn after that tend to be learned faster, always depending on the difficulty of the language.
